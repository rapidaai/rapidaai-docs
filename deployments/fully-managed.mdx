The Rapida Fully Managed option is for teams that need enterprise reliability and fast rollout without owning the infrastructure. It is a strong fit for agencies launching multiple client programs quickly and enterprise teams that want to focus on assistants, not platform operations.

## Key Benefits

- **Zero Infrastructure Management**: No server provisioning, monitoring, or maintenance
- **Automatic Updates**: Always run the latest version with security patches and new features
- **Enterprise-grade SLAs**: 99.9% uptime guarantee with 24/7 monitoring
- **Dedicated Support**: Priority technical support from our engineering team
- **Flexible Scaling**: Resources automatically adjust based on your usage patterns
- **Regional Deployment Options**: Optimize latency and governance requirements across multiple regions

## When to Choose Fully Managed

The Fully Managed option is ideal for:
- Agencies that need to launch multiple client programs without building a platform team
- Enterprise teams without dedicated DevOps ownership
- Applications that need enterprise-grade reliability
- Projects requiring rapid time-to-market
- Companies focused on product delivery rather than infrastructure operations
