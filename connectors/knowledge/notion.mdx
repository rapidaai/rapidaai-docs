---
title: "Notion"
description: "Connect your Notion workspace to Rapida to use pages and databases as knowledge sources for your AI assistant."
---

The Notion connector syncs pages and databases from your Notion workspace into Rapida's knowledge base. Your assistant can then retrieve and answer questions based on your Notion content.

## Prerequisites

- A Notion account with access to the workspace you want to connect
- The Rapida administrator must have configured `OAUTH2__NOTION_CLIENT_ID` and `OAUTH2__NOTION_CLIENT_SECRET` in the environment

## Connecting Notion

Follow these steps to connect Notion to Rapida:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Connectors page">
    In the Rapida dashboard, navigate to **Integrations → Connectors** and select **Notion** under Knowledge Connectors.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Rapida">
    Click **Connect**. You will be redirected to Notion's authorization screen. Grant Rapida `read_content` access to the pages and databases you want to share.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select content to sync">
    After authorizing, choose the Notion pages and databases to include in your knowledge base. Only content explicitly shared with the integration during the authorization step will be visible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Attach to a knowledge base">
    Navigate to the knowledge base where you want to use this content, click **Add Source**, and select your Notion connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

After authorization, Rapida queries the Notion Search API (`POST https://api.notion.com/v1/search`) using your workspace token. It retrieves all pages and databases that were shared during the OAuth flow, sorted by last edited time.

Content is indexed into your knowledge base and used for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) when your assistant answers questions.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `read_content` | Read pages and databases in your Notion workspace |

## Limitations

- Only content explicitly shared with the Rapida integration during authorization is accessible. Rapida cannot access content that was not shared.
- Syncing large workspaces may take a few minutes.
- Changes to Notion content require a manual re-sync to be reflected in the knowledge base.
