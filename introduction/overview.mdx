Rapida Voice AI is the first open source voice AI orchestration platform that allows you to run enterprise grade production voice AI systems. It provides a comprehensive stack including LLMs, TTS, STT, knowledge base integration, CRM connectivity, telephony, noise cancellation, observability, and telemetry - all in a single unified platform.

## Why choose Rapida Voice AI Platform?

Rapida provides a complete solution for voice AI applications with several key advantages:

- **Fully open source**: Complete transparency with all components available as open source.
- **End to end voice stack**: Integrates LLMs, STT, TTS, knowledge bases, and telephony integrations in one platform.
- **Observability**: Comprehensive monitoring, logging, and telemetry for voice AI systems.
- **Model flexibility**: First-class support for integrating any LLM, STT, TTS models into voice experiences.
- **Scalable**: Designed to support from a handful of calls to thousands of concurrent interactions.
- **Deployment flexibility**: Choose between fully-managed cloud or self hosted options.
- **Private and secure**: End-to-end encryption, compliance, and robust data privacy controls.

## Rapida Voice AI Platform components

The Rapida Voice AI platform consists of these core components:

- **Voice Orchestration Engine**: The central open source server that coordinates all components of the voice AI system.
- **LLM Integration**: Seamless connectivity with leading language models (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Cohere, etc).
- **Speech Processing**: Enterprise grade STT and TTS with noise cancellation.
- **Knowledge Base**: Tools for creating and querying knowledge bases for AI assistant.
- **Telephony**: Complete toolkit for integration with inbound or outbound calling systems.
- **Observability**: Comprehensive monitoring, logging, and analytics for voice AI performance.

## Deployment options

Rapida provides two deployment options for Voice AI Platform to fit your needs:

- **Managed Cloud**: A fully-managed, globally distributed service with automatic scaling and high reliability. Trusted by companies of all sizes, from startups to enterprises.
- **Self hosted**: Run the open source Rapida Voice AI Platform on your infrastructure for maximum control, customization, and data sovereignty.

Both options provide the same enterprise grade features and use the same SDKs.

## What can you build with Rapida Voice AI Platform?

- **Customer Service Voice AI**: Build voice assistants that handle customer inquiries with human-like conversation.
- **Healthcare Voice Systems**: HIPAA-compliant telehealth with AI features for patient support.
- **Sales & Support**: Intelligent voice agents for outbound and inbound sales and support calls.
- **Multilingual Voice AI**: Deploy voice assistants that communicate fluently across languages.
- **IVR Replacement**: Replace traditional IVR systems with conversational voice AI.
- **Voice Chatbots**: Integrate sophisticated voice capabilities into existing chatbot systems.
